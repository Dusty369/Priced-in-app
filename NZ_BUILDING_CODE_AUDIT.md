### ✅ B2/AS1 - Durability (Timber Treatment Levels)
**Source:** NZ Building Code Performance Requirements
**Coverage in App:** YES
**Treatment Levels Specified:**
- **H1.2** - Interior framing, dry, no weather exposure ✅
- **H3.1** - Exterior above ground, painted/coated ✅
- **H3.2** - Exterior above ground, uncoated (decks, pergolas, fences) ✅
- **H4** - Ground contact (posts in concrete/soil) ✅
- **H5** - In-ground structural, freshwater contact ✅

**Verdict:** ✅ Correct treatment levels assigned to appropriate applications

## Additional NZ-Specific Standards Verified

### ✅ Fencing Standards
- Post spacing: 2.4m-2.7m ✅
- Post depth: 600mm minimum (1/3 of length) ✅
- Pool fencing height: 1200mm ✅
- Pool gate self-closing/latching ✅
- Gap restrictions: under 100mm ✅

### ✅ Deck Standards
- Joist spacing: 450mm standard (400mm max for 19mm boards) ✅
- Post embedment: 600mm minimum ✅
- Decking: 140x32mm, 140x25mm, or hardwood 140x19mm ✅
- Under-deck clearance considerations ✅

### ✅ Retaining Wall Standards
- Under 1.5m: timber acceptable ✅
- Over 1.5m: engineering required ✅
- Drainage: 100mm gravel + ag pipe ✅
- Post embedment: 1/3 of wall height minimum ✅
